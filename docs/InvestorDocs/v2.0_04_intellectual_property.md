## IP Risks & Mitigation

### Risks

1. **Patent Rejection**: USPTO may reject patent application
   - **Mitigation**: Strong prior art search, experienced patent attorney
   - **Probability**: 20%
   - **Impact**: Medium (trade secrets still protect)

2. **Trade Secret Leakage**: Employee or competitor theft
   - **Mitigation**: NDAs, access controls, monitoring
   - **Probability**: 5%
   - **Impact**: High (core technology)

3. **Infringement Claims**: Competitor claims we infringe
   - **Mitigation**: Prior art search, design around
   - **Probability**: 10%
   - **Impact**: Medium (legal costs, delays)

4. **Reverse Engineering**: Competitors reverse engineer algorithm
   - **Mitigation**: Obfuscation, server-side processing
   - **Probability**: 15%
   - **Impact**: Medium (takes 2-3 years)

### Overall IP Risk: **Low to Medium**

**Rationale:**
- Strong patent application
- Comprehensive trade secret protection
- Limited exposure (server-side processing)
- First-mover advantage (3-5 year head start)
